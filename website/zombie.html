About Virtual Reality?

Virtual Reality (VR) technologies, have the potential to become the next big computing platform. All around us are examples of where VR can reshape existing ways of doing things – from buying a new home to interacting with a doctor or watching a concert.

VR technology heightens the videogame experience by completely immersing a user in a virtual world. We see VR videogames as the first consumer market to develop as we see both the hardware and software further along in development as well as the gamer community eagerly awaiting the technology.

Story

Dead-city-runner is a VR game product developed by our Team. The story begins after a global viral pandemic wipes out over 80% of the world population, causing the dead to rise and feed on the living. In a matter of months, society has crumbled; there is no government, no Ralphs, no Amazon Prime delivery and no WIFI.

Luckily, we have a bunch of smart scientists and professors here at University of Trojans. They are working days and nights to develop a novel vaccine to cure this dead disease. Unfortunately, they don’t have enough time. About 10,000,000 Zombies are coming from the highway I-110N, they will arrive in no time. In order to defend our lab and make more time, a brave CS graduate student steps out and volunteers to take this suicide mission. He will use all the weapons given by the campus DPS and survive as long as he could to save the lab and world. God bless our young hero.

Game Features

  • The user can play the game on iOS and Android using Google Cardboard.
  • The player travels automatically through its environment and must shoot Zombies coming towards it. The Cardboard trigger button will shoot.
  • 3 gun choices: pistol, rifle, fire gun; The player can change weapon by looking down and clicking the menu. Different level of demage will be caused to zombies depends on different gun.
  • There will be games' information in the looking down's menu, e.g. change weapon, health, survival time, restart and pause.
  • The player can shoot the power up package to recover their health.
